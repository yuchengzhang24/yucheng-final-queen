Queen

"I always knew I was a star, and now the rest of the world seems to agree with me."

We are the Champions

Queen are a legendary British rock band formed in 1970; their lineup consisted of Freddie Mercury, Brian May, Roger Taylor, and John Deacon. Quickly rising to fame, they became the most prominent stadium rock band by the early 1980s - with many hits including "We Are the Champions" and "We Will Rock You." They have since become one of the world's best-selling music artists.
